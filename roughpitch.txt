What problem we solve
- Graduates look for high-profile startups with the greatest opportunity for growth and creative input

What Unicorn.io is
- Scrape user resume and generate a normalized word frequency vector
- Scrape company information from crunchbase.com and generate a normalized word frequency vector for each company
- The word frequency profile for each company is compared with that of the user to find the best focus/interest match
- Our algorithm also prioritizes company size, funding round to select companies
- We use our own pdf scraping technology and our own api for extracting company information

Why Unicorn.io is important
- Anyone can instantly find personalized recommendations for compatible startup companies, enabling graduates to have more control over their careers
